Company Profile:


Sunshine Energy Inc. Group is one of the leading energy and engineering solutions provider, offering a range of products and services that help customers effectively meet their energy needs in a safe and sustainable manner. Our highly qualified Business executive work closely with our Investors to help them secure maximum returns out of their investments. Our strategic plan of action and infrastructure enables fast growth and secured returns we work closely with our clients to ensure our customers receive full Value for their investments.
The Company is associated with various National Bodies such Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (Government of India) & Solar Energy Corporation of India to serve customers with highest quality standard The company is driven by its core values of building enduring relationships and its vision to be a trendsetter in energy solutions. Our integrated energy and engineering solutions portfolio ensure our customers remain at the forefront, now and into the future.
Sunshine Energy is a Company engaged in Design, Consultancy, Integration, Supply, Installation, Commissioning and Maintenance of all types of on- grid and off-grid Solar Power Projects with market leading brand. Our engineering team is highly experienced and have expertise in reducing electricity losses. With turnkey solutions – right from installation to lifetime maintenance – we ensure your journey towards sustainability is a smooth one. Our prompt legal and financial closure and commissioning processes are set in place to minimize any hassles along the way. All you need to do is provide your idle roof or unused land at your site, and we will do the rest.



OUR BUSINESS PARTNERS


Havells Private Limited


HPL Electric and Power Limited ("HPL"), a multi-product electric equipment company, is an established player in the electrical equipment industry. The Company has always believed in being technically abreast and keeping focus on the design and development of products. HPL has two R&D centers, with over 100 engineers for designing & development modern products. the R&D Centers are approved by the Department of Scientific and industrial Research, Ministry of Science and Technology, Government of India. HPL Group, was established in 1956 as "Havells Private Limited". In 1974, another group company "Havells Electronics Private Limited" came into existence. In 1971, the group adopted a new logo as "HPL". Since then, HPL Group has carved a niche for itself in the electrical industry globally. The flagship company HPL Electric & Power Ltd was incorporated in 1992.
